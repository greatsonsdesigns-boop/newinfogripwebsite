Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our social media services

How quickly will I see results?

Most clients notice increased engagement within the first 2-3 weeks. Significant growth in followers and reach typically happens within 60-90 days as we optimize content based on performance data. We provide weekly updates so you can track progress from day one.

Which social media platforms do you manage?

We specialize in Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and Twitter/X. Based on your business and target audience, we'll recommend the most effective platforms for your goals. Our most popular package is Instagram + Facebook management, which works well for most businesses.

Do I need to provide content or ideas?

Not at all! We handle everything from content creation to posting and engagement. However, we welcome your input and will work closely with you to ensure the content aligns with your brand voice and business objectives. We'll schedule a kickoff call to understand your brand thoroughly.

Can I cancel if I'm not satisfied?

Yes, we offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with our service within the first 30 days, we'll provide a full refund. After that, you can cancel anytime with a 30-day notice. Our goal is to deliver such great results that you'll want to stay with us long-term.

How do you measure success?

We track multiple metrics including follower growth, engagement rate, reach, website clicks, and lead generation. Every month you'll receive a detailed report showing exactly what we've accomplished and how it's contributing to your business goals. We focus on metrics that actually matter for your bottom line.
