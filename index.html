Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes

What services does InfoGrip provide?

We offer comprehensive digital marketing services including social media management, website development, political & personal branding, and ads campaign management. Our team creates tailored strategies to help businesses grow their online presence and achieve their marketing goals.

How does your onboarding process work?

Our onboarding process is simple and efficient. After an initial consultation, we'll send you our onboarding form to gather necessary information about your business, goals, and preferences. Once completed, we'll create a customized strategy and begin implementation within 3-5 business days.

Do you offer custom pricing?

Yes, we offer custom pricing based on your specific needs and budget. We provide transparent pricing with no hidden costs. After understanding your requirements, we'll create a customized package that fits your goals and budget constraints.

How long does it take to see results?

The timeline for seeing results varies depending on the service and your industry. Generally, you can expect to see initial improvements within 2-4 weeks, with more significant results appearing after 3-6 months of consistent implementation. We provide regular progress reports to keep you informed.

Do you handle political branding?

Yes, we specialize in political branding and have experience working with political candidates and parties. Our services include developing comprehensive branding strategies, creating compelling messaging, managing social media presence, and running targeted ad campaigns to reach voters effectively.

Do you provide website maintenance?

Absolutely! We offer comprehensive website maintenance services including regular updates, security monitoring, performance optimization, and content updates. We provide flexible maintenance plans to ensure your website remains secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally at all times.

Can I get monthly social media management?

Yes, we offer monthly social media management packages that include content creation, posting, engagement, and performance reporting. Our packages are customizable based on your needs, and we can manage one or multiple platforms depending on your requirements.
