Our Journey, Our Vision

InfoGrip Media Solution began with a simple idea — to help brands grow in a digital-first world. Founded by two young entrepreneurs Nimesh Prajapati and Ritik Kumar Prajapati, InfoGrip has quickly evolved into a performance-focused digital agency trusted by businesses, startups, and public figures.

From social media management and branding to website development and political digital strategy — our goal is simple: deliver measurable results with honesty, creativity, and innovation.

We believe in transparency, long-term partnerships, and building digital ecosystems that truly work.
