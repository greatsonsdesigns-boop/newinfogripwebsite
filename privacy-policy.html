Introduction

At InfoGrip Media Solution, accessible from https://www.infogrip.in, we are committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring transparency in how we collect, use, and safeguard information. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of data we collect, how it is used, and your rights regarding your information.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information

    Name, email address, phone number, and other details voluntarily provided by you.

  • Usage Data

    Pages visited, time spent on site, IP address, browser type, and device information.

  • Cookies & Tracking Technologies

    We may use cookies, analytics tools, and tracking pixels to enhance user experience and improve website performance.

  • Form Submissions

    Any information submitted through contact forms, onboarding forms, or inquiry forms.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To communicate with you regarding inquiries or support
  • To personalize your website experience
  • To analyze website performance
  • For advertising and remarketing (Meta, Google, etc.)
  • For internal record keeping

Note: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data with third parties.

Cookie Policy

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to monitor website activity and store certain information. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, although this may affect some features of the website.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, WhatsApp API, and email marketing tools. These services may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no online platform can guarantee 100% security.

Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Opt-out of marketing communication
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details provided below.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us to remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised 'Last Updated' date.
