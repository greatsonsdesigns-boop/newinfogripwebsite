Introduction

At InfoGrip Media Solution, we strive to provide excellent digital and branding services. This Refund & Cancellation Policy outlines how refunds, cancellations, and payment terms are handled for all clients using https://www.infogrip.in.

General Policy

All refunds and cancellations are subject to the terms mentioned below. By purchasing our services, you acknowledge and agree to these policies.

Service-Based Refund Guidelines

  • Digital Marketing, Website Development, Political Branding, SMM, and Creative Services

    Refunds are not provided once work has started, including:

    • Strategy planning
    • Designing
    • Content creation
    • Scheduling
    • Initial research
    • Any communication regarding the project
  • Advance Payments

    All advance payments or booking amounts are non-refundable due to resource allocation and time investment.

  • Custom Packages

    If a client requests a custom plan or special service, refunds do not apply once any part of the work begins.

Cancellation Policy

Client-Initiated Cancellation

Clients can request cancellation before work begins. If the project has not started, a partial refund may be considered (after deducting administrative charges).

Cancellation After Work Begins

Company-Initiated Cancellation

If InfoGrip Media Solution cancels a project due to unforeseen reasons, clients will receive a proportional refund for the remaining work.

Non-Refundable Items

  • Completed work or deliverables
  • Services already performed
  • Design drafts, copywriting, graphics, or edited videos
  • Monthly service fees (political branding, SMM, ads, etc.)
  • Paid advertising budget (if client has funded ads)
  • Third-party tool or subscription payments

Turnaround Time & Delays

Any delay caused due to the client (late content, pending approvals, incomplete requirements) will not qualify for cancellation or refund.

Payment Disputes

In case of billing disputes, clients must contact us within 3 days of payment. We will review each request individually, but claiming a refund after service completion is not permitted.

Changes to This Policy

We may update or revise this Refund & Cancellation Policy at any time. The updated version will be posted on this page with the latest 'Last Updated' date.
