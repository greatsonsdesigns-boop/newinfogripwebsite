Political Branding & Marketing Services

What is political branding and how is it different from normal marketing?

Political branding focuses specifically on building a leader's public identity, credibility, and voter connection through digital channels. Unlike general marketing, it involves reputation management, narrative control, constituency communication, and election-focused positioning. Our Political Branding service creates a consistent digital persona that resonates with voters and builds lasting political capital.

Which political services do you provide exactly?

We offer comprehensive political digital solutions including:

  • Political Branding: Digital identity, image building, and online reputation management
  • Political Social Media Framework: Structured content planning and platform management
  • Digital Election Campaigns: End-to-end campaign execution with voter outreach
  • Campaign Creative Design: Visual content, posters, and campaign materials
  • Political Ads Management: Meta and Google Ads for political visibility
  • WhatsApp Voter Outreach: Targeted constituency communication
Who can use your services? Do you work with new politicians?

We work with political leaders at all levels – from local councilors and municipal candidates to MLAs, MPs, and party organizations. Yes, we actively work with new politicians who want to establish their digital presence from scratch. Our onboarding process includes foundation building, audience identification, and gradual visibility scaling suitable for first-time candidates.

What makes your political marketing approach unique?

Our approach combines political understanding with data-driven digital execution. We focus on measurable voter connection, compliance-aware strategy, and scalable systems that work during both routine periods and election campaigns. Unlike generic agencies, all our processes are built specifically for political communication requirements and electoral timelines.

Political Ads & Campaign Management

Are political ads legal in India? What about Meta authorization?

Yes, political ads are legal in India with proper disclosure and compliance. For Meta platforms (Facebook/Instagram), we require:

  1. Government-issued ID of the candidate/authorized representative
  2. "Paid for by" disclaimer with name and address
  3. Meta's authorization process completion
  4. Ad library transparency labeling

We handle the complete authorization process and ensure all ads comply with platform policies and Indian regulations.

How specific can you target with political ads?

We use advanced targeting options:

  • Geographic: Exact assembly constituency, ward, polling booth areas
  • Demographic: Age, gender, language, education level
  • Interest-based: Political affiliations, community groups, local issues
  • Custom Audiences: Website visitors, previous engagers, voter lists
  • Lookalike Audiences: Similar to your existing supporters

All targeting respects platform policies and maintains voter privacy.

What is the typical timeline for a digital election campaign?

Our Digital Election Campaigns follow this timeline:

  • Phase 1 (Pre-announcement): Brand building, issue positioning (2-3 months)
  • Phase 2 (Campaign start): Candidate introduction, manifesto communication (1 month)
  • Phase 3 (Peak campaign): High-frequency ads, voter outreach, event promotion (45 days)
  • Phase 4 (Final week): GOTV efforts, polling day information, final push
  • Phase 5 (Post-election): Thank you messages, result communication
What results can I expect from digital campaigns?

Typical outcomes include:

  • 3-5x increase in social media engagement
  • 2-4x growth in follower base during campaign
  • 50,000-5,00,000+ constituency reach per week
  • 15-30% lower cost per voter contact vs traditional methods
  • Real-time feedback on issues and public sentiment

Important: Digital campaigns complement ground efforts; they don't replace door-to-door campaigning or local networks.

Legal, Compliance & Ethics

How do you ensure Election Commission compliance?

We maintain strict compliance through:

  • All political ads include mandatory "Paid for by" disclaimer
  • Expenditure tracking as per ECI guidelines
  • Adherence to model code of conduct periods
  • No surrogate advertising or hidden promotion
  • Transparent reporting for election expense affidavits
  • Regular updates on changing election regulations

We consult with political compliance experts to stay updated on all requirements.

What are your content responsibility policies?

We follow strict content guidelines:

  • No false/misleading claims about opponents
  • No hate speech, communally sensitive content
  • Fact-checking of all statistics and claims
  • Respect for all communities and religions
  • No personal attacks or character assassination
  • Compliance with platform hate speech policies

We reserve the right to refuse content that violates these policies, even if approved by client.

How do you handle data privacy and voter information?

We adhere to Indian data protection norms:

  • Voter lists used only for legitimate political communication
  • No selling/sharing of contact databases
  • WhatsApp broadcasts follow TRAI regulations
  • Opt-out mechanism in all communications
  • Secure storage of all client information
  • NDA protection for sensitive campaign data

We believe ethical data practices build long-term voter trust.

What is your stance on political neutrality?

InfoGrip operates as a professional digital execution partner. We:

  • Do not endorse any political party or ideology
  • Work with clients across the political spectrum
  • Maintain strict confidentiality between clients
  • Focus on execution, not political strategy
  • Respect all constitutional democratic processes
  • Maintain professional boundaries in all engagements

Our expertise is digital communication, not political consultancy.
